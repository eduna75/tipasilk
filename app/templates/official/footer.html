About Tipasilk

Tipa silk comes from the Isaan part of Thailand. The silk is produced by locals and hand woven into scarfs and fabrics that’s been used to make the finest clothes. Isaan is pronounced e- saan and is a big area of Thailand that’s connected to Cambodia and Laos.
